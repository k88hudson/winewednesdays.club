Mozilla Toronto
Wine Wednesdays Club

Love wine? Want to try something new? We meet in the Mozilla Toronto office most Wednesdays at 5:00pm.

Join our mailing list

If you want to receive weekly calendar invites, announcements, and a list of all the wines we try, subscribe to our mailing list: moto-wine-club@mozilla.com

Donations

We appreciate donations as we are mostly self-funded. You can send money online to paypal.me/katehudson. If you have a mozilla.com email, you can check our financial records as of Feb 2017 here.

Visiting the Toronto office?

Visitors and friends are always welcome! If you are planning on bringing a larger group (such as a work week) contact Kate Hudson (:k88hudson) or host@winewednesdays.club

Want to bring wine?

Awesome!!! Contacting Kate Hudson (:k88hudson) or host@winewednesdays.club to let us know what you're bringing will help with coordinating our other wines / cheeses.
